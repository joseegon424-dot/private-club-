Everything you need to know about membership, rules, and our club culture.

A social club is a private association where members can gather to consume cannabis in a safe, legal, and regulated environment. Access is restricted to registered members only. Our club provides a comfortable space for relaxation and community building.

Anyone who is at least 21 years old and holds a valid government-issued ID (Passport or EU ID card) can apply for membership. We reserve the right of admission and all applicants must go through our verification process.

You can apply directly through our website on the "Join Club" page. Fill out the application form with your details. Once your application is reviewed, you will be invited to the club to finalize your registration and pay the membership fee.

The membership fee is €20 per year. This contribution helps maintain the club's facilities, services, and community events for all members. The fee is payable in cash during your first visit.

We are open every day from 12:00 PM to midnight (00:00). We recommend checking our social media for any special holiday hours or events that may affect our schedule.

Members can sponsor new members, but all visitors must complete the full membership process before entering. We do not allow non-members to enter the club premises. Each guest must register and pay the membership fee.

We provide a comfortable lounge area, smoking accessories (bongs, vaporizers), gaming consoles (PS5, Xbox), board games, music, and a relaxed atmosphere. We also host regular events like Dab Nights and gaming tournaments.

In Spain, private cannabis social clubs operate in a legal gray area. Consumption within our private premises is tolerated as it takes place in a private, members-only environment. Public consumption remains illegal. All cannabis at the club is for personal use only.
